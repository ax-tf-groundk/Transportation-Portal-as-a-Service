Busan — Gimhae Int'l Airport (PUS)

The most convenient gateway, serving domestic and international flights from major cities across Asia, with direct connections from 26 cities in 7 countries (as of April 2026) including Beijing, Hong Kong, Osaka, Shanghai and Tokyo.

✈️

Seoul — Incheon Int'l Airport (ICN)

Korea's largest airport. Travelers can reach Busan within 2–3 hours by either a domestic flight or the KTX high-speed train.

✈️

Seoul — Gimpo Int'l Airport (GMP)

A major domestic airport in Seoul with convenient flights to Busan and other Korean cities; also serves selected international routes.

2 Incheon (ICN) → Gimhae (PUS) ▼
  • Korean Air operates 5 to 6 daily flights between Incheon (ICN) and Gimhae (PUS), depending on the day of the week.
  • These are international transfer flights and can only be purchased as part of an itinerary linked to an international ticket (via Korean Air and its codeshare partners).
  • As international flights, transfers take place at Incheon Airport Terminal 2, and immigration clearance is completed at Gimhae Airport (PUS).

5 Direct Bus from Incheon (ICN) → Haeundae ▼

There are direct bus connections from Incheon International Airport to Busan. Travel time is approximately 5–6 hours; limousine bus tickets can be purchased at the on-site ticket office or in advance via the T money-Go mobile app (Korean phone number required). Ticketing — [Terminal 1] indoor Exits 4–11 / outdoor Exits 3–11; [Terminal 2] B1 Transportation Center.

Baggage Information
  • Each passenger may bring up to 2 free pieces of baggage (total up to 30 kg).
  • 25 inches or larger is limited to 1 piece per person.
  • Additional or oversized baggage (3rd piece and above) is charged at 50% of the adult fare per item.
  • The carrier is not responsible for storage or compensation for baggage exceeding the allowed limits.

Subway — Travel time approx. 70 min · Fare approx. KRW 2,300 (tickets at machines or the customer service center).

  • Route: Gimhae International Airport Station (Busan–Gimhae Light Rail) → Sasang Station (transfer to Line 2) → Centum City Station (Line 2)

Airport Limousine Bus — Travel time approx. 60 min · To Haeundae / Gijang.

Taxi — Travel time approx. 60 min (may vary) · Fare approx. KRW 40,000 (night surcharge may apply) · Location: Domestic Terminal 1F Gate 2. ※ Use official taxi stands only.
