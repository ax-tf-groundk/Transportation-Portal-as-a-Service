Getting to Busan
How to reach the city from major gateways.
✈️

Busan — Gimhae Int'l Airport (PUS)

The most convenient gateway, with direct connections from 26 cities in 7 countries including Beijing, Hong Kong, Osaka, Shanghai and Tokyo.

✈️

Seoul — Incheon Int'l Airport (ICN)

Korea's largest airport. Reach Busan within 2–3 hours by domestic flight or KTX high-speed train.

✈️

Seoul — Gimpo Int'l Airport (GMP)

Major domestic airport in Seoul with convenient flights to Busan; also serves selected international routes.
