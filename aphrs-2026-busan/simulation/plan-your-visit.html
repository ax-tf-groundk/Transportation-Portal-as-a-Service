Plan Your Visit

Key practical information to help you prepare for a smooth trip to Busan.

☀️

Climate

October brings mild, sunny autumn weather — one of the best times to visit. Average 15℃ – 22℃ (59℉ – 72℉).

₩

Currency

Korean Won (KRW). Major credit cards widely accepted and ATMs available throughout the city.

🔌

Electric Outlets

Type C & F plugs, 220V / 60Hz. Visitors with other plug types may need an adapter.

🕘

Time Zone

KST (Korea Standard Time, UTC+9). Please check the difference with your local time zone.

💬

Language

Korean is official; English is common in tourist areas. Hello = 안녕하세요, Thank you = 감사합니다.

🏪

Business Hours

General 09:00–18:00 · Banks 09:00–16:00 (weekdays) · Malls 10:30–20:00 · Restaurants ~11:00–22:00.

📶

Wi-Fi & SIM

Free Wi-Fi at BEXCO, hotels, cafés and public spaces. SIM / pocket Wi-Fi available at the airport.

💳

Tipping

Tipping is not customary. Service charges are typically included at hotels and upscale restaurants.

🛡️

Insurance

Participants are advised to arrange travel and health insurance before departure. The Organizing Committee provides no coverage.

🆘
Safety & Emergency

South Korea is a very safe destination. Standard precautions for personal belongings are recommended.
