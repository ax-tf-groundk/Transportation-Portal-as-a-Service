Welcome to K-Food!

Korea has developed a rich and distinctive culinary culture shaped by its mountains, plains, and surrounding seas. Korean cuisine is known for its vibrant presentation—staple dishes such as rice, noodles, and vegetables are prepared using a variety of traditional cooking methods that emphasize balance and nutrition. Celebrated for both its flavor and healthfulness, Korean cuisine has gained global recognition.

The Essence of Korean Cuisine

As the host city of APHRS 2026, Busan offers a vibrant blend of coastal beauty, dynamic city life, and distinctive cuisine. This unique atmosphere is naturally reflected in the city's food culture. From fresh seafood to hearty street food, Busan offers a variety of flavors and culinary experiences.

To further enrich your stay during APHRS 2026, we are pleased to highlight a diverse selection of menus from outstanding restaurants across the city. We hope this adds to your visit.
