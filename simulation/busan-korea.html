Explore Busan

A vibrant port city of beaches, culture, and unforgettable food on Korea's southeast coast.

Busan Highlights
📍 Haeundae Beach

Busan's most famous beach with beautiful sea views and a lively atmosphere.

📍 Gwangalli Beach

Stunning night views of Gwangan Bridge and seaside cafés.

📍 Gamcheon Village

Colorful hillside art village with charming alleyways.

📍 Haedong Yonggungsa

Seaside Buddhist temple set against dramatic cliffs.

🏖️

Beaches

Haeundae, Gwangalli, Taejongdae cliffs, and Busan Tower views.

🏛️

Heritage

Beomeosa Temple, Haedong Yonggungsa, and the UN Memorial Park.

🛍️

Shopping

Shinsegae Centum City, Nampo-dong, and Jagalchi seafood market.

🎬

Culture

Busan Int'l Film Festival (BIFF), G-STAR, and Gamcheon Village.

Taste of Busan K-Food
Must-try local flavors during your stay.
🍲

Dwaeji-gukbap

Busan's signature pork-and-rice soup — hearty and local.

🍜

Milmyeon

Chewy cold wheat noodles, a Busan summer favorite.

🦑

Jagalchi Seafood

Fresh fish, crab, and octopus at Korea's largest seafood market.

🥟

Ssiat Hotteok

Seeded sweet pancakes — an iconic Nampo-dong street snack.
