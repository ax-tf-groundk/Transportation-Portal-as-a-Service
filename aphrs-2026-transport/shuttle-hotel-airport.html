Information · 이용 안내

Usage Guidelines

Operations & Booking

  • This is a paid, individually booked shuttle for APHRS 2026 participants departing from Busan.
  • Departure dates: Oct 24 (Sat) – Oct 25 (Sun), 2026. Vehicle: 45-seat coach. Frequency: every 30 minutes (confirmed schedule provided at booking).
  • Please arrive at the pickup point at least 5 minutes before your scheduled departure time.
  • Seats are not pre-assigned — first come, first served on board.
  • Real-time vehicle tracking is not available. Actual arrival times may vary due to traffic conditions.
